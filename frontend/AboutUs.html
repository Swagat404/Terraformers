The Terraformers Team
The Terraformers team comprises Natasha Kamara, Mariesandra Kibisu, Arok Bul, Swagat Bhowmik, Neelab Wafasharefe, Mike Zhang, and Arvand Kianimoghadam. Natasha, Neelab, and Swagat primarily worked on the frontend and Unity development, bringing the visual and interactive aspects of the project to life. Arok focused on setting up permanent storage solutions and managing the database systems. Marie worked on the AI avatar brains. While Mike and Arvand worked on backend development.
